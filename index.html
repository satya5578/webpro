NAME: GEORGE REDDY

DATE OF BIRTH:15 January 1947

INDIAN RESEARCHER STUDENT IN PHYSICS AT OSMANIA UNIVERSITY

  • Reddy was an Indian researcher student in physics at Osmania University, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

  • He is mostly known for his helping nature, and his huge enthusiasm for nuclear physics made him to get his gold medal

  • His untimely death led to the formation of the Progressive Democratic Students Union (PDSU), a student body on the campus of the University that took its name from the PDS imprint he had used to publish pamphlets
  • Reddy was killed in an attack at his college campus on 14 April 1972.

  • A 152-pages book named Jeena Hai To Marna Seekho:The Life and Times of George Reddy written by Gita Ramaswamy was published in 2016 by Hyderabad Book Trust

  • A Telugu film titled George Reddy was released in 2019

known for Leading a socialist movement and Founder of PDSU

DIED 14 April 1972 (aged 25)
